You met us at the door every single time —
no questions asked, no conditions.
Just joy. Pure and unconditional.

Some presences are so constant, so quietly woven into the fabric of every day — that when they're gone, the silence they leave behind is deafening. That's Taica.

The mornings that still expected paws on the floor. The evenings that expected weight against your leg. The loyalty that never asked to be earned — only given, without hesitation, every single day.

This page exists so he is never reduced to just a memory. So his name stays somewhere permanent. So anyone who finds this knows — he was real, he was loved, and he mattered more than words can hold.

"Not all dogs change your life.
Some of them just are your life."
